The Absolute Best Movies Ever

Just Kidding, Just my Opinion

Star Wars: Revenge of the Sith

The Star Wars films were some of the first movies I ever watched growing up and it would be incredibly foolish for me to not include this title in my top rankings. Space fantasies are also just superior, sorry not sorry.

The Twilight Saga: Eclipse

The Twilight Saga is fantastically bad, and it is entirely because of that that any of the movies are making their way onto this list. I'm also just a huge fan of vampires, so I can't help that I love it.

A Silent Voice

This movie is an absolutely amazing animated film about the impacts of bullying and the importance of found family, and I think it does an amazing job of shedding light on how often those with disabilities are bullied.
